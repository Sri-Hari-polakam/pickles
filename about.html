The Heart Behind Suravi

Born from a deep-rooted passion for authentic flavors, Suravi Pickles started by our founder, **P. Kavya Sri**, in a small traditional kitchen in vijayawada. Inspired by generational recipes, she set out to bring the true essence of Indian spices—patience, sunlight, and love—to every household.

Today, we continue that legacy. Every jar you order is crafted by hand under Kavya's supervision, entirely homemade without any artificial preservatives. We guarantee supreme hygiene, incredible quality, and an authentic taste that transports you straight to your childhood.

  • 100% Traditional Handcrafted Recipes
  • Premium Locally Sourced Organic Spices
  • Uncompromising Hygiene & Safety Standards
